Thermal Themes

Premium Shopify themes for specialty retail.

Studio

Thermal Themes is a small Shopify theme studio. We make themes for merchants who sell things that reward research — gear, instruments, tools, components, the categories where buyers cross-shop on specifications and want technical information treated as content rather than ornament.

Our themes pair editorial design discipline with the feature depth a working store needs. We build slowly, ship deliberately, and serve narrower categories than the multi-purpose themes that dominate the Theme Store.

In development

A theme for specialty audio retailers — headphones, amplifiers, speakers, components. In active design and development. Theme Store submission expected in 2026.
